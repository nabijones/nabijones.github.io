Korea D-234

Following a strong pull I could not explain, I boarded a one-way flight to South Korea with two suitcases full of dreams. What began as an exciting adventure turned into an unexpected journey of self-discovery. Korea became both a mirror and a teacher, revealing every hidden part of me, every wound that still needed to heal.

Korea D-234 is a memoir of personal transformation set against the backdrop of South Korea's vibrant, soulful culture. It is a story that weaves through relationships, family, health, identity, and the longing to belong. A reminder that our deepest struggles can guide us home to ourselves and the life we are meant to live.

About Me

Through personal experience, I write about the wounds we survive, the dreams we chase, and the joy we find along the way.

My writing reflects a life lived across cultures, a love for languages, and my background as an AI researcher. At its core, it honours human values whether I write about soul, science, or the bridge between the two.

Between the words I share, I hope to hold a space for you to pause, breathe, and feel. A space where healing begins and inner transformation unfolds.
